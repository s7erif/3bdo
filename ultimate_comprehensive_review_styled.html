Hypothyroidism

Hypothyroidism is deficiency of thyroid hormones causing generalized slowing of metabolism. In children it is critical for brain development and growth.

Physiology

  • CNS development and myelination.
  • Linear growth and skeletal maturation.
  • Increase basal metabolic rate.
  • Increase heart rate and cardiac output.
